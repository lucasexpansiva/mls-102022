invoiceGeneratorPage

Objective

Allow administrators to create, customize and export invoices in PDF to clients quickly and securely, maintaining compliance with basic tax rules.

Usage Tips

  • Start by selecting or adding a client, providing name and email to enable sending the invoice.
  • Add invoice items with quantity, unit price and tax rate; totals are calculated automatically.
  • Generate a PDF preview before downloading or sending by email; choose template language (EN/PT/ES) for localized invoices.
